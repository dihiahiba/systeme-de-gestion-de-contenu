About

MyPress is a simple flexible and easy-to-use.
MyPress has two major components:
  • a content management application (CMA), as the front-end user interface that allows a user, even with limited expertise, to add, modify, and remove content from a website without the intervention and assistance from a web developer;
  • and a content delivery application (CDA), that compiles the content and updates the website.

Technology is best when it brings people together.
